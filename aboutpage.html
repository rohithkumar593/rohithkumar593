About Sri Venkateswara University

Sri Venkateswara University,Tirupati is established in 1954 on the sprawling campus of 1000 acres with a panaromi and pleasant hill view.The university stands as a testimony to the wisdom and foresight of great Visionaries 'Late Sri Tanguturi Prakasham Pantulu,the then chief minister of Andhra and',Late Sri Neelam Sanjiva Reddy to catter the educational needs and aspirations of the people.
